About Me

Hello!

I like building projects and machines, and having a little too much fun doing it.

Engineering

I have experience working with the Vex V5 Robotics System and competiion (2360S/2360H)

I also have taken several courses on engineering, introducing the engineering design process and applying it to product design

I have worked firsthand with circuitry, circuit simulation softwares, AOI Logic and implementation, as well as proficiency with CAD software (Fusion360/Onshape)

Games!

I'm currently working on two solo games, Lysis and Chronomancer (Read more about them in the Projects section!)

Lysis is an action rouguelike where you take the role of a bacterial cell, fighting the immune system

Chronomancer is an action metroidvania, with an emphasis on manipulating time to gain the upper hand

I am also a developer for a group project, Bread Quest, a platformer.

I have worked with python, C++, HTML, and Gdscript. These projects are all made in GDscript, with the godot game engine.

Music

I also write the soundtrack to both the solo projects mentioned above.

I have been writing music for ~3 years (as of 6/7/26), and mainly work with orchestral and symphonic metal

I originally started writing music in musescore for fun, but eventually switched to FL Studio as tracks got increasingly more complex.

I have released many remixes, and have been working on original music and soundtracks for 2 years.
